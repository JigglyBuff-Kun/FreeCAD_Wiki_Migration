## Descrição

The ![](/images/BIM_TDView.svg) [BIM TDView](/BIM_TDView "BIM TDView") tool creates a new TechDraw view from a section plane or 2D objects.

## Utilização

1. There are several ways to invoke the tool:
   * Press the ![](/images/BIM_TDView.svg) [View](/BIM_TDView "BIM TDView") button.
   * Select the **Annotation → ![](/images/BIM_TDView.svg) View** option from the menu.
