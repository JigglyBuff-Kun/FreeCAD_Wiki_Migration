## Description

Applies a hull to selected shapes.

![Example of a hull operation performed on two cylinders](/src/assets/images/Hull_example.jpg)

Example of a hull operation performed on two cylinders

## Usage

1. Select shapes in [Tree view](/Tree_view "Tree view") or in [3D view](/3D_view "3D view")
2. Click on ![](/src/assets/images/OpenSCAD_Hull.svg) or choose OpenSCAD → ![](/src/assets/images/OpenSCAD_Hull.svg) Hull from the top menu.

## Limitations

Feature needs OpenSCAD installed and configured in Edit → Preferences
